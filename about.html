About FreeStyle Decorators

Born from passion, driven by creativity — we're Patna's most trusted balloon and event decoration artists.

Our Story

Started with a Balloon, Built on Dreams

FreeStyle Decorators was born in Patna, Bihar, with a simple belief — every celebration deserves to be extraordinary. From a single balloon arch to full wedding stage transformations, we've grown into one of Patna's most recognised decoration brands.

Located near Old Arvind Mahila College in Kadamkuan, we've served hundreds of families, colleges, and corporate clients across Patna and beyond. Our team blends artistic creativity with technical precision to create setups that take your breath away.

What sets us apart? We don't just decorate — we curate experiences. Every setup is personalised to match your vision, your colours, and your story.
