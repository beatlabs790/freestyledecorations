Have Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

How early should I book the decoration? ▼
We recommend booking at least **3 to 5 days in advance** for small birthday parties, and **2 weeks in advance** for large wedding stages or corporate events. This helps us ensure availability and procure custom elements (like neon name signs or specific pastel colors). Same-day slots may be available in emergency cases; please call us directly.
How long does the setup process take? ▼
A standard balloon garland or room setup takes about **1.5 to 2.5 hours**. Large stage setups, mandaps, or organic balloon walls can take **3 to 5 hours**. Our team arrives on-site early to ensure the setup is completed cleanly and on time before your guests arrive.
Do you decorate outdoor venues? ▼
Yes, we decorate both indoor halls and outdoor settings (gardens, terraces, lawns). For outdoor balloon setups, we use heavy-duty metal frameworks and premium, double-stuffed latex balloons to ensure they withstand direct sunlight and wind without popping.
Are your packages fully customizable? ▼
Absolutely! Every package listed on our site is fully customizable. You can add extra balloons, change color palettes, add fresh flower accents, include neon light signs, custom printed cutouts, or rent specific metal structures. We will adjust the custom quote accordingly.
What locations in Patna do you serve? ▼
We are based in **Kadamkuan** and serve **all locations across Patna, Bihar** including Boring Road, Kankarbagh, Bailey Road, Raja Bazar, Saguna More, Patna City, Patliputra, and Danapur. Transportation charges are included in our premium package quotes.
